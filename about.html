Our Story

Born from Love.
Built for Remembrance.

EverPaws was built around a simple truth: the love between a person and their dog deserves to be honoured. When a dog passes, what families need most is something real — something they can hold, something that carries a piece of who their companion truly was.

That's the idea at the heart of everything we make.

"Grief is just love with nowhere to go. We wanted to give that love a home."

EverPaws is a small studio dedicated entirely to helping pet parents carry their dog's memory with them every single day — beautifully, meaningfully, and with love.

The Process

How Your Keepsake is Made

Every EverPaws capsule is made with intention, care, and a deep respect for the bond you shared.

1

Choose Your Capsule

Browse our collection of beautifully crafted acrylic fur capsules and select the one that feels right for your dog.

2

Personalize It

Add your dog's name, dates, and a short message to be printed on your handwritten gift card.

3

Include Their Fur

When your order arrives, simply place a small lock of your dog's fur into the hidden chamber inside the capsule.

4

Carry Them Always

Attach to your keys, wear as a pendant, or gift to someone who is grieving. They are with you, always.

Our Values

Every decision we make comes back to the same question: what would bring the most comfort to a grieving pet parent?

💛

Compassion First

We understand that purchasing a memorial is an emotional act. We approach every order with deep empathy and care for what you're going through.
