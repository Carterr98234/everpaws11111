Our Story

Born from Love.
Built for Remembrance.

EverPaws was built on a simple belief: the bond between a dog and their family deserves to be honoured in a way that lasts. When a dog passes, pet parents deserve something real to hold on to — not just a photo, but a piece of who their companion truly was.

"Grief is just love with nowhere to go. We wanted to give that love a home."

That's the idea behind our fur keepsake capsules. Each one is a transparent acrylic sculpture with a hidden chamber inside to hold a small lock of your dog's fur. Carry them on your keys, wear them as a pendant, or display them somewhere close.
