FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our keepsakes, ordering process, and how to make the most of your memorial.

How do the fur keepsake capsules work?

+

Each EverPaws capsule is a beautifully detailed acrylic sculpture of your dog's breed. Inside the sculpture is a small hidden chamber designed to hold a lock of your dog's fur. When your order arrives, you simply open the chamber (instructions included), place a small amount of fur inside, and close it. The capsule then becomes a wearable, carriable memorial.

How much fur do I need to include?

+

Only a very small amount — roughly the size of a coin. The chamber is intentionally compact to keep the capsule elegant and wearable. You can use fur collected from a brush, grooming, or saved from a haircut. If you don't have fur available, we also offer a "Gift Mode" option where we include a soft placeholder and instructions so the recipient can fill it themselves.

How long does shipping take?

+

Orders typically ship within 1–2 business days and arrive within 3–5 business days (domestic). International orders take 7–14 business days. We know how meaningful these orders are, so we prioritize processing as quickly as possible. You'll receive tracking information as soon as your order ships.

Can I get a refund or exchange?

+

Absolutely. We offer a 30-day satisfaction guarantee on all orders. If your keepsake arrives damaged, isn't as described, or simply isn't right for you — contact us and we'll arrange a replacement or full refund, no questions asked. We want you to feel completely at peace with your purchase.

Do you offer customization (names, dates, messages)?

+

Yes! Every order includes a personalized gift card printed with your dog's name, birth and passing dates, and a short memorial message of your choosing (up to 100 characters). This card is tucked inside the gift box so you or the recipient can keep it alongside the keepsake.

What breeds are currently available?

+

We currently offer: Corgi, Schnauzer, Golden Retriever, Yorkshire Terrier, Pomeranian, Husky, Westie (West Highland Terrier), and Shiba Inu. We're continuously adding new breeds — if yours isn't listed, contact us and we'll let you know when it's coming, or add you to our breed waitlist.

Is the packaging gift-ready?

+

Yes — every EverPaws order ships in beautiful packaging: a matte gift box, a velvet pouch for the capsule, a personalized gift card, and a handwritten note from our team. No extra steps needed — it's ready to give exactly as it arrives.

Do you ship internationally?

+

Yes, we ship worldwide. International delivery typically takes 7–14 business days. Shipping costs are calculated at checkout based on destination. All international orders include tracking. Please note that import duties may apply depending on your country.
